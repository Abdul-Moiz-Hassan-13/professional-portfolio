Final Year Project

Memora - AI Healthcare Augmentation System

An AI-powered healthcare augmentation system designed to improve doctor–patient consultations.

Project Overview

An AI-powered healthcare augmentation system designed to improve doctor–patient consultations by helping doctors remember patient history, automate consultation notes, detect emotional cues, and guide patients after visits. Memora combines face recognition, speech-to-text, AI summarization, sentiment analysis, prescription analysis, and clinical decision support to make healthcare interactions more accurate, continuous, and personalized.

Workflow & Key Features

1
Patient Recognition

Patients are automatically identified upon arrival using a face recognition pipeline to match records securely.

Face Recognition Authentication
2
Profile Preloading & History

Loads the patient’s medical history, allergies, prescriptions, and previous summaries to generate a dynamic memory card for the doctor.

Medical Memory Card Profile Ingestion
3
Real-time Transcription

During consultations, the system records and transcribes the conversation between doctor and patient in real time.

Speech-to-Text Whisper Integration
4
AI Summarization

Generates a structured clinical consultation summary from the transcript and automatically updates the patient record.

LLM Summarization EHR Autoupdate
5
Sentiment & Emotion Analysis

Detects stress, anxiety, hesitation, or emotional cues dynamically, giving doctors cues for patient-centered care.

Sentiment Detection Emotion AI
6
Clinical Suggestions & Alerts

Provides doctors with context-aware clinical reminders, risk flags (e.g. allergies, drug-drug interactions), and diagnostic assistance.

Decision Support Risk Alerting
7
Prescription Analysis (OCR)

Analyzes physical prescription images via OCR and translates complicated medical jargon into simple, patient-friendly explanations.

Prescription OCR Jargon Translation
8
Integrated Portals & Dashboards

Unified dashboards for doctors, patients, and admins. Supports journey tracking, booking appointments, and secure messaging.

Doctor, Patient, Admin Portals Secure Chat
